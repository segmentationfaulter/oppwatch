An urdu language website built for doing a POC with Astro. The website will be more like a news website. We will be having two content collections in this website (one for global news, other for news from Palestine). Both the collection will have:

- their own pages, where links of the articles belonging to that collection will be listed.
- their own specific area on home page, where we will list the links of five latest articles of that collection

Every article should have the followings:

- A title
- An author
- Time when it is published
- A hero image
- Content of the article
